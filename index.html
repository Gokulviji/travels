"Welcome to Wanderlust"

Wanderlust travels Pvt. Ltd is a subsidiary of Sangam Group of Hotels, one of the biggest hotel chains in south Tamil Nadu. The Sangam Group of Hotels founded in 1968 has for forty years provided impeccable service and outstanding hospitality. It currently has 19 properties in Tamil Nadu. GT Holidays Pvt. Ltd built on this strong foundation aims to provide great customer satisfaction and an exemplary holiday experience. Planning a once in a lifetime holiday or a yearly corporate retreat? No problem!
